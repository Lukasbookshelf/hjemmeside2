Illustrations:

This was a bookcover I created to my own webseries The Rise Of The Assassin. The story is a dark fantasy story, which is why the cover needed to match the tone of voice in the story.

I drew the sword in ProCreate, and used illustrater to make dublicate it for the bookcover.

Pictures:

Writing:

I have participated in NaNoWriMo twice, and both times I won. NaNoWriMo stands for National Novel Writing Month and takes place every november.

NaNoWriMo is a global writing competition where you have to write a novel within a month.

I've always been a writer. when I was little I wouldn't leave the house without a notebook and a pencil. Writing has been a difficult sometimes since I'm dyslexic, but it won't stop me..
